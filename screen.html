AmpliTube 5 Tone Switcher

Automatic MIDI preset switching synced to song playback

How it works

When you load a song, the plugin extracts its tones from the song file, converts them to AmpliTube 5 presets in real time (~50ms), and automatically sends MIDI Program Change messages to AT5 as each tone change fires during playback. The Tone Browser tab lets you preview any song's tones and manually trigger them. The Live Log captures every tone switch during your session.
